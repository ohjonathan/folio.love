## 7. Verdict
Revise and re-review

Justification: While the author successfully addressed B-1 and B-3, the resolution for B-2 (operationalizing queue bounds) introduced a new technical gap. The reframed pending-queue state cap is implementable, but the new rolling acceptance-rate gate requires a chronological history of reviews. Because `folio/links.py` currently timestamps confirmations but not rejections, calculating the "20 most recently reviewed" proposals across the workspace is not operationalizable without new persistence infrastructure. The author must either adjust the design to include rejection timestamps or redefine the rate gate to not require global historical ordering.
